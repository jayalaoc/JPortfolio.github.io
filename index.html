Who's Josue?

I’m from the south side of Cali, the sunny city of San Diego. I have been living here for a substantial amount of my life and consider this place my home. As the years went by, I realized contributing to my community was something I found very fulfilling. It took me about four years, hard work, a university degree to begin my career as a Data Scientist in the Behavioral Health Services Department. If you know me, you will see that I always have much to say and questions to ask for learning is a lifelong endeavor.
